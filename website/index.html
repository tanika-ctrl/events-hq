Who we are

Beyond the technical —
we think like a creative partner.

Events HQ specialise in delivering high-impact production, AV and lighting solutions for every kind of event — from corporate presentations and film & TV shoots to virtual events, public events and more.

We don't do "off the shelf." Every brief is unique, and so is our approach. Whether you're planning a product launch in a London ballroom, a live broadcast on set, or a hybrid conference in a historic venue, our team designs and delivers bespoke setups tailored to your goals, audience and environment.

Our role extends beyond the technical. We bring strategic thinking and operational expertise to the table, helping clients navigate complex production environments while staying firmly aligned with creative goals, brand tone and audience expectations.
